FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is SSI and who qualifies? +
Supplemental Security Income (SSI) is a federal program providing financial assistance to individuals who are aged 65+, blind, or disabled with limited income and resources. Our specialists can help determine if you qualify and guide you through the process.
What types of loans does MABRY assist with? +
We assist with personal loans, emergency loans, and other financial products. Our team assesses your situation and connects you with the most appropriate loan solution based on your needs and eligibility.
How do I know if I qualify for an SSI upgrade? +
Many SSI recipients are receiving less than they're entitled to due to errors or outdated information in their file. Our specialists conduct a thorough review of your case to identify any opportunities for an upgrade. The best way to find out is to apply and let us take a look.
Is there a cost to use MABRY's services? +
MABRY does not charge any upfront fees. We are committed to transparency and integrity — you will never be asked to pay before your loan is approved or your benefit upgrade is confirmed.
How long does the entire process take? +
Timeframes vary depending on the service and your individual situation. Most initial reviews are completed within 24 hours. Full loan approvals typically take 1–3 business days. SSI upgrades may take longer depending on the complexity of your case.
