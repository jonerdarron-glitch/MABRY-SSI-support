FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does the loan approval process take? +
Most applications are reviewed within 24 hours of submission. Complex cases may take 2–3 business days. We keep you updated at every stage.
Are there any upfront fees? +
Absolutely not. MABRY does not charge any upfront fees prior to loan approval and disbursement. We pride ourselves on full transparency and integrity.
Is my personal information kept confidential? +
Yes. All client information is handled with the highest level of confidentiality and used solely for verification and processing purposes. We never share your data with third parties.
Can I apply if I'm already receiving SSI benefits? +
Yes. Many of our clients are already receiving SSI and come to us for upgrade support or loan assistance. Our specialists will review your current benefit status and identify the best path forward.
What documents do I need to get started? +
Requirements vary by service, but generally you'll need a valid ID, proof of address, and any relevant SSI notices or financial statements. Our team will guide you through exactly what's needed.
